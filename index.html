Game Rules

  • Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.
  • Agree ahead of time whether you'll count "rock, paper, scissors, shoot" as just "rock, paper, scissors."
  • Use rock, paper, scissors to settle minor disputes or simply play to pass the time.
  • If both players lay down the same hand, that player has neither hand.
  • The game is played by two players.
  • The scores increment for each win.
  • The scores are stored in your browser and persist across reloads.
  • A celebration animation appears when you win.
